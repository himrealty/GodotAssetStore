Install CGLive

2 steps • No computer needed • 2 minutes

1

📱 Turn off Play Protect (temporary)

1. Open Play Store app
2. Tap your profile picture → Play Protect
3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
4. Turn OFF "Scan apps with Play Protect"
⚡ Don't worry — you can turn this back on after installing
2

📦 Install via Termux (1 command)

1. Install Termux from F-Droid: ⬇️ Download Termux 2. Open Termux and copy-paste this:
pkg install -y termux-api && termux-open /sdcard/Download/app-debug.apk
3. When installer opens → tap "Install"
✓ That's it! App is installed
(You can re-enable Play Protect now)
